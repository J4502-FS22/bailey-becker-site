The Harry Potter movies, in order from worst to best

The world was first introduced to a mythical world with witches and wizards in 1997 when author J.K. Rowling released the first Harry Potter book. When the book series was finished it had seven books and was later adapted into 8 films. The following list ranks the 8 movies from worst to best.

Harry Potter And The Order Of The Phoenix

Directed by: David Yates

This is the fifth movie in the series and was released in 2007. Harry and his friends are in their fifth year at Hogwarts and lord Voldemort is back! A new teacher named Dolores Umbridge is hired at the school and refuses to teach defensive spells. Harry then creates a secret group called Dumbledore’s army and teaches this group how to do defense spells.

Harry Potter And The Order Of The Phoenix on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Prisoner Of Azkaban

Directed by: Alfonso Cuarón

This movie is the third in the series and was released in 2004. The gang is in their third year at Hogwarts and begins to uncover Harry’s connection to the recently escaped Prisoner Sirus Black. Harry also begins to meet people from his parent's past such as Professor Lupin, a werewolf and Peter Pettigrew who had been disguised as Ron’s pet rat.

Harry Potter And The Prisoner Of Azkaban on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows - Part 1

Directed by: David Yates

This movie is the seventh in the series, covers the first half of the last book and was released in 2010. At the beginning of the movie Minster of Magic has just been killed and the ministry has fallen. Harry and his friends then discover that Lord Voldemort has placed his sole into horcruxes and they embark on a mission to find them and destroy them.

Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows - Part 1 on imdb.com

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Directed by: Chris Columbus

This movie is the first in the series and was released in 2001. The film begins with Harry being introduced to the World of magic where he meets his friends Ron, Hermione and Hagrid. A professor at the school named Quirinus Quirrell is trying to find a magical stone called the sorcerer’s stone. Harry is able to stop him but discovers that lord Voldemort’s been living on the back of his head under a turban.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Chamber Of Secrets

Directed by: Chris Columbus

This movie is the second in the series and was released in 2002. Students at Hogwarts began being petrified and no one could figure out why. Harry and his freinds later discover that these students are being petrified because they indirectly saw a monster called a basilisk (a type of snake). They then discover a secret place called the chamber of secrets where the basilisk had been living.

Harry Potter And The Chamber Of Secrets on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Goblet Of Fire

Directed By: Mike Newell

This movie is the fourth in the series and was released in 2005. Hogwarts announces that they are hosting a Triwizard tournament with three other magical schools. Harry’s name is placed in the goblet of fire (a magical goblet used to decide who will compete in the tournament). The four contestants go through three stages of competitions with Harry later winning the tournament.

Harry Potter And The Goblet Of Fire on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows - Part 2

Directed by: David Yates

This was the eighth and final movie in the series and was released in 2011. Harry and his friends battle lord Voldemort's army and they are finally able to defeat him. But this battle kills many who were close to Harry. The very end of this film then shows Harry, Ron and Hermione's children attending Hogwarts later 19 years later.

Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows - Part 2 on imdb.com

Harry Potter And The Half-Blood Prince

Directed by: David Yates

This movie is the sixth in the series and was released in 2009. Harry receives a potions book that was once owned by the half-blood prince that teaches him a few things. Ron falls in love and Hermione gets jealous mean while Harry starts to develop feelings for Ginny.
